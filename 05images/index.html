Your Source for Festival News

Music festivals have become insanely popular worldwide in the past century, but they weren’t always as we know them to be today. The origins of music festivals date back to Ancient Greece, with events that involved competitions in music and arts as well as sports. It was a celebration of culture, and over the years, elements have been added and changed to become the extravagant productions we now experience.

Here at FestiDesti, we introduce you to and educate you on events here in the United States from the East Coast to the West Coast and everything in-between, as well as the rest of the world. We can also help you find outfits, accessories, safety tips, and festival do’s and don’ts. Think of us as your Festi Fam, helping you have the best experiences you can imagine.

Festival Hacks

  • Bring your own water bottle.
  • Don't forget sun protection.
  • Wear sturdy shoes.
